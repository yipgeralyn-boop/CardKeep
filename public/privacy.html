Privacy Policy

DueCard  ·  Last updated: June 9, 2026

DueCard ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app:

  • Email address — collected when you create an account, used solely for authentication (sign in / sign out / password reset).

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, payment details, or any financial credentials.

Card Data

All credit card information you enter (card names, balances, due dates, APR) is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

No Bank Access

DueCard never connects to your bank, requests banking credentials, or accesses any financial accounts. You manually enter the information you choose to track.

Authentication

We use Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) to manage sign-in. Your email address is stored securely by Firebase. Google's privacy policy applies to data handled by Firebase: policies.google.com/privacy.

Subscription

DueCard Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment or billing information.

Data Retention

Your account and associated authentication data remain until you delete your account. You may request deletion by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

DueCard is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
